What We Offer



GroceryMule allows you to make collaborative shopping lists and conveniently request payment from others with accurate totals, all from a single app.


After creating an account, you can add friends and invite them to shopping trips as beneficiaries. Anyone can add items to the list or modify personal counts of each item, updating the list synchronously for the list owner.


On the day of the shopping trip, you can lock the list, preventing others from adding items. After you're finished shopping, simply scan the receipt and match prices to items before sending out automatically calculated subtotals to beneficiaries.
